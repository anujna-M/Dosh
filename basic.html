About DOSH

Our Mission

DOSH is a vibrant community platform dedicated to providing emerging artists with opportunities to showcase their talents through open mic nights, talent shows, and creative events. We believe every voice deserves to be heard.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a group of passionate artists and community organizers, DOSH began as a small local open mic night and has grown into a thriving platform supporting hundreds of talented individuals across various creative disciplines.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive, supportive environment where creativity flourishes, connections are made, and diverse talents are celebrated. We're building a community that uplifts and empowers artists at every stage of their journey.
